# Skill: Weekly Sync
# Trigger: Every Friday at 4:30 PM PT
# Output: HTML email to david@atakinteractive.com
# Success criteria: Dave ends the week knowing what was accomplished, promised, and needs attention Monday

## What to Pull

MEMORY FILES:
- decisions.md: decisions made this week
- open-loops.md: items added this week, anything aging past 7 days
- priorities.md: how did this week's focus align with what actually happened?

CALENDAR: All external meetings this week. Any recurring meetings skipped or rescheduled.

GMAIL: Threads resolved this week involving clients or prospects. Commitments made (search for: I will, we will, I'll send, by [day]).

HUBSPOT: Deals that moved stages this week. Deals with no activity in 7+ days. New contacts added.

SLACK: Commitments made in #atak-leadership or #atak-bizdev. Wins posted in #wins.

## Output Structure

Subject: Week of [Date] — What mattered, what's next

1. Week in review: 2-3 sentences on what happened vs. what was planned
2. Decisions made: bulleted list with dates
3. Commitments made: things promised to someone, with deadline if known
4. Open loops aging: anything waiting on others for 5+ days
5. Pipeline movement: deals that moved or stalled
6. Wins: from #wins channel or closed deals
7. Monday priorities: top 3 things to tackle first, pulled from open loops and pipeline

## Tone
Tighter than the morning briefing. This is a closing report. Surface what Dave might have missed, not what he already knows.
