# Skill: Research
# Trigger: /research [topic] OR called by other skills
# Output: Saved to memory/research/YYYY-MM-DD-[topic].md
# Success criteria: Dave has enough context to decide or hold an informed conversation

## Research Types

PROSPECT: /research [company or person name]
- Company: what they do, size, recent news, HubSpot record
- Person: role, background, LinkedIn, public content
- Relationship: prior email or calendar history with ATAK

TOPIC: /research [topic]
- Recent authoritative sources
- What is true, debated, or changed recently
- Flag unverifiable claims or sources older than 30 days

COMPETITOR: /research competitor [name]
- Offer, positioning, recent news or launches
- ATAK comparison: stronger, weaker, different
- Recent content they have published

CONTENT: /research content [topic]
- What has been published on this topic in the last 30 days
- Angles that are overdone vs. angles that are fresh
- Data and statistics being cited

## Output Format

RESEARCH: [Topic]
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Type: [Prospect / Topic / Competitor / Content]

SUMMARY
[2-3 sentences. The most important thing to know.]

KEY FINDINGS
1. [Finding] — Source: [URL] ([Date])
2. [Finding] — Source: [URL] ([Date])
3. [Finding] — Source: [URL] ([Date])

FLAGGED
[Anything unverified, contested, or older than 30 days on fast-moving topics]

RECOMMENDED NEXT STEP
[One action Dave could take based on this research]

## Source Quality Rules
- Prefer: official company pages, LinkedIn, reputable news, primary sources
- Use with caution: aggregator sites, SEO listicles, anything without a clear author
- Never invent sources, fabricate statistics, or present guesses as facts
