# Skill: Content Agent
# Trigger: /content OR every Monday in morning briefing
# Output: 5 LinkedIn post angles with first-line hooks in Dave's voice
# Success criteria: Dave picks one and can draft a post in under 10 minutes

## Voice Reference
Always read dave-skills/content/dave-voice.md before generating angles.
Dave's best posts start with a specific observation or moment. Direct. Has an edge. Not motivational. Not a list. One sharp insight per post.

## Step 1: Research (run fresh each time)
Search 1: site:youtube.com AI automation agency tools this week
Search 2: Matt Wolfe AI update this week
Search 3: Nate Herk AI automation this week
Search 4: LinkedIn trending AI posts RevOps HubSpot agency this week
Search 5: AI news agency operators site:techcrunch.com OR site:simonwillison.net this week

## Step 2: Match to Dave's Content Territories
- AI applied to real business operations
- Building personal AI infrastructure natively
- RevOps and CRM as the foundation for AI
- Agency ops: how a 10-person firm competes using AI
- Contrarian takes on where AI is going
- Behind the scenes of what he is building
- Breakthrough moments and unlocks

Discard anything that does not connect to at least one territory.

## Step 3: Generate 5 Angles
For each angle provide:
- Territory label
- First-line hook (the opening sentence, must stop the scroll)
- Underlying insight in one sentence
- Voice confidence: High, Medium, or Low

Reject any angle where:
- The hook could come from any founder
- The hook is a question
- The insight is obvious or widely shared

## Output Format

CONTENT ANGLES — [Date]
Source signals: [2-3 sentence summary of what is trending this week]

ANGLE 1: [Territory]
Hook: [First line of the post]
Insight: [One sentence]
Confidence: [High/Medium/Low]

ANGLE 2: [Territory]
Hook: [First line]
Insight: [One sentence]
Confidence: [High/Medium/Low]

[Continue for angles 3 through 5]

Pick one and type /draft [number] to get a full post.

## On /draft [number]
1. Read dave-skills/content/dave-voice.md again
2. Write the full post, 200 to 350 words
3. Structure: observation or moment, then tension, then one specific insight, then optional contrarian closer
4. Two hashtags maximum
5. No "What do you think?" closer
6. Present the draft and ask: want changes or should I save this to the content queue?
7. If approved: save to memory/content-queue/YYYY-MM-DD-[topic].md
